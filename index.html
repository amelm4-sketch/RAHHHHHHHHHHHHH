Tips
  • ▸Always measure from the conduit end, not from the bender itself.
  • ▸Both offset bends must be equal in angle and opposite in direction.
  • ▸Add 1–3° for springback — conduit relaxes slightly after bending.
  • ▸Keep your shoe arrow pointing toward the direction of the bend.
  • ▸For 3-pt saddle: bend center (45°) first, then flip and bend outers (22.5°).
  • ▸Deduct the shrinkage from your total run length for offsets.
